Advisory services

Maintaining a property is a herculean task for a homeowner. Whether you wish to buy a new property, to sell your property, or even rent out your existing property, we at Loyal Global offer you our expertise to help you in a prompt and resolution-driven way. We ensure due diligence and validation on your behalf if you are selling, buying, or renting your property. You can also choose a broker of your choice through whom all the procedures will be initiated and accomplished. Loyal Global can take care of a number of integral tasks pertaining to your property. This also includes the marketing of your property by posting ads on the relevant property portals. We can also help you with the setting up of a ‘To Let’ or ‘For Sale’ board at your property and then in dealing with any and all enquiries that come for the property. Whether it is the requirement of a property valuation, assistance for getting a bank loan, need of expert opinions or references from a legal advisor, or even negotiation assistance, Loyal Global’s Advisory Services have been tailored to ensure that your entire property management experience is smooth, systematic and completely hassle-free.
